Substation’s mystery fire brings chaos

Hundreds of homes, businesses, and large parts of the transport network were plunged into chaos due to a fire in an electricity substation, for the second time in under a fortnight. The second mystery fire in Cunningham Place and Aberdeen Place, Lisson Grove, took down a chunk of the Bakerloo, Waterloo and City, Suffragette and Weaver lines on the London Underground and Overground on Monday afternoon.
